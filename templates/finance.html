Forecasting Closing Prices of Financial Equities

March 28, 2024

Hi team,
The data we will be using is generously provided by Yahoo Finance. We will look at the closing price of equities by day. The data availible varies depending on the equity's maturity. The goal is to fit a forecasting model to predict the trend for equities of personal interest; we will utilize the Prophet Model to do so. Most notably, I've created an interface for you to input your ticker symbol and forecast length so that you can observe and explore the forecasted trends and prediction metrics computed by the Prophet model. You can look up ticker symbols here.

This model is notorious for its accuracy and flexibility. However, the volatility of closing prices vary depending on the equity itself, so the responsible user will invoke their personal judgement and use this merely as a tool to compliment (or contradict) their personal claims.

Note: I limitted the start date to 5 year prior in order to help mitigate computational cost with the added benefit of predictive accuracy. Although you may ask the model to forecast far into the future, I recommend looking at more immediate forecasts using small time intervals such as days and months. You will notice, the farther into the future the model predicts, the wider the uncertainty invterval is, indicating less reliable predictions.
